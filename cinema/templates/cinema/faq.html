❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about bookings, loyalty rewards, or theater formats? Find quick answers below.

🎟️ How do I book tickets on CineVerse? ▼
Booking tickets is simple! Browse the available movies under **Browse** or **Now Showing**, select a film to view details, pick an available showtime, choose your seats (VIP, Normal, or Couple), apply any promo vouchers at checkout, and confirm your booking.
💳 What payment methods are supported? ▼
We support safe and instant online payment processors including **Momo**, **ZaloPay**, and major **Credit Cards** via our integrated payment gateway adapters.
⭐ How does the loyalty membership work? ▼
Every booking earns you membership points! Points accumulate to level up your status tier from **Bronze** -> **Silver** -> **Gold** -> **Platinum**. Higher tiers enjoy discounted ticket rates and exclusive lounge/IMAX promotions.
🎬 What do the age rating badges stand for? ▼
Our age rating labels represent standard film classification rules:
  • P: General audience (Suitable for all ages).
  • C13: Restrictive to viewers aged 13 or older.
  • C16: Restrictive to viewers aged 16 or older.
  • C18: Restrictive to viewers aged 18 or older.
🍿 Can I cancel or refund my ticket? ▼
Yes! You can request a ticket cancellation up to **60 minutes** before showtime. Refunds are processed automatically back to your member wallet balance or original payment adapter.
